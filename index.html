About Us

Arshan Traders is a proprietorship founded by Mohammed Arshan, built on a foundation of quality, integrity, and customer satisfaction. Located in Pernambut, Tamil Nadu, we have a deep-rooted commitment to serving our community with products that offer both protection and precision.

Our mission is to support our clients' success by providing high-grade leather products and ensuring their financial compliance through our specialized services. We combine traditional craftsmanship with modern efficiency to deliver excellence every time.

With years of experience in leather manufacturing and business services, we understand the diverse needs of our clients and strive to exceed their expectations with every product and service we deliver.

Our Products

Leather Hand Gloves

We produce a wide variety of leather hand gloves tailored for different applications, focusing on protection, durability, and comfort.

  • Industrial Gloves: Designed for demanding environments like welding, construction, and manufacturing
  • Welder's Gloves: Excellent heat resistance and dexterity for safe work
  • Materials: Cowhide, pigskin, and goatskin for strength and flexibility

Leather Aprons

Our leather aprons are crafted to offer robust protection and long-lasting performance across various professions.

  • Welding & Blacksmith Aprons: Shield against sparks, heat, and molten metal
  • Woodworking Aprons: Protect from dust, splinters, and tools
  • Features: Adjustable straps, reinforced stitching, and multiple pockets
